Key takeaways

Electronics drives 56% of total revenue from just 22% of orders — a high-value, low-volume segment.

Central region delivery time (5.6 days) sits 1.5 days above every other region, correlating with its lowest average rating (3.87).

Direct traffic has the strongest repeat-customer rate (44.8%) despite not being the top revenue channel.

Revenue is seasonal — Sep–Nov 2025 shows clear month-over-month acceleration into the holiday peak.
